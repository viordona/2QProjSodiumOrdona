How It Works

Select

  • Choose from the great selection of luxury bags.
  • Bags that are not available are labeled â€œOn Loan."
  • Click "Reserve Now" to start your transaction and to be redirected to ORDER ONLINE .

Order Online

  • Pick a delivery date and how long you would like to rent it for.
  • Provide all information requested.
  • Make payment. Payment options are via online banking
    or Gcash only.

When You Receive Your Bag

  • Your bag will be in a dust bag inside a padded hard box.
  • It will have an attached tag with return instructions and guidelines for its care.
  • If you are not satisfied with your order, DO NOT REMOVE THE TAG.
  • Returns are only accepted if the tag has not been removed.

When You Are Ready To Return Your Bag

  • Place the bag in the dust bag and then into the box that
    it came in with.
  • Take the box to your nearest JRS service.
  • Attach the return label that came with the package in the
    box JRS will provide.
